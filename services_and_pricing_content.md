#### Bookkeeping Pricing
The monthly price is calculated using a base rate derived from transaction volume and account count, optionally doubled for accrual accounting, plus specific add-ons.

**Formula:**
`((Transaction Volume Base + Account Count Fee) × Accounting Method Multiplier) + Low/High Complexity Add-ons`

**1. Transaction Volume (Base Amount)**
*   0 - 300 transactions: **$250**
*   301 - 600 transactions: **$300**
*   601 - 1000 transactions: **$500**
*   1001 - 2000 transactions: **$1000**
*   Over 2000 transactions: **$1500**

**2. Number of Accounts**
*   Up to 5 accounts: **+$0**
*   5 - 10 accounts: **+$50**
*   Over 10 accounts: **+$150**

**3. Accounting Method Multiplier**
*   Cash Basis: **1x** (No change)
*   Accrual Basis: **2x** (Doubles the sum of Transaction Base + Account Fee)

**4. Add-ons (Added after multiplier)**
*   Merchant Processors (Stripe, Square, etc.): **+$100**
*   Loans/Leases to track: **+$50**
*   Depreciable Assets:
    *   0: **+$0**
    *   1 - 5: **+$50**
    *   6 - 10: **+$100**
    *   Over 10: **+$200**
